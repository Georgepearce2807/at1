Radio Telecommunications

Radio Telecommunications (Ratel), a method of communicating over a radio used in the Australian Army and Australian Army Cadets. Ratel is used in most field exercises and has a strict code that must be followed


Common Radio Procedures

Casualty Evacuations (CASEVACS), Location Statements (LOCSTATS), and Radio Checks are all common radio procedures carried out whilst participating in a Ratel Activity
